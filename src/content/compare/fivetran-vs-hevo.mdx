# Fivetran vs Hevo Data: Which Is Better in 2026?

**Short answer (verified April 2026):** Choose **Fivetran** if you're syncing 100M+ monthly active rows into Snowflake, BigQuery, or Databricks and need enterprise-grade connector coverage for SAP, Oracle, NetSuite, or Salesforce. Choose **Hevo Data** if your team is sub-50 person, needs predictable flat-rate pricing starting at $299/mo, and values a visual no-code pipeline builder over transformation depth. Fivetran wins on scale, connector reliability, and warehouse-native patterns. Hevo wins on cost predictability for small-to-mid workloads and onboarding speed for teams without a dedicated data engineer. Neither is a fit for sub-minute real-time use cases — both are batch-oriented.

## When to Choose Fivetran

- **You run SAP, Oracle EBS, or NetSuite alongside Salesforce.** Fivetran's ERP connectors are the most mature on the market as of Q1 2026. Hevo's ERP coverage is thinner — verify specific connector availability with vendor before committing.
- **You're syncing 100M+ MAR/month into Snowflake, BigQuery, or Databricks.** At this scale the MAR model is generally cheaper than Hevo's events-based tiers, though exact crossover depends on row churn patterns.
- **You already use dbt.** Fivetran's posture is "land raw data, transform in dbt." This is the dominant modern-data-stack pattern and integrates cleanly with dbt Cloud.
- **You need a 99.9% uptime SLA in writing.** Fivetran publishes this on Enterprise; Hevo's SLA terms are not publicly disclosed as of April 2026.
- **You're replacing brittle scheduled SQL exports or Power Automate flows.** Fivetran's managed CDC eliminates the "Monday morning broken refresh" class of incidents.

## When to Choose Hevo Data

- **Your monthly data volume is under ~10M events and budget is fixed.** The $299/mo Starter tier is predictable; Fivetran MAR billing at low volume can be cheaper *or* more expensive depending on row update patterns — harder to forecast.
- **You don't have a data engineer.** Hevo's visual pipeline builder is genuinely easier for analysts coming from Power Query, Alteryx, or Excel-based workflows.
- **You need lightweight in-pipeline Python transforms without standing up dbt.** Hevo supports this natively; Fivetran pushes transforms downstream.
- **You're a stepping-stone team.** Hevo works well as a 12-24 month bridge before a formal data platform buildout. Migration to Fivetran later is straightforward (both write to the same warehouses).

## Migration Notes

Switching between Fivetran and Hevo is mechanically low-risk since both load to the same warehouses (Snowflake, BigQuery, Databricks, Redshift). Budget 1-2 weeks per 10 connectors for re-authentication, historical backfill, and dbt model validation. The hardest part is reconciling schema differences — Fivetran and Hevo normalize source tables slightly differently, so downstream dbt models usually need light adjustments.

## Alternatives to Both

- **Airbyte** — Open-source ETL; ~60% cheaper at volume if you can absorb ~4 hrs/month of ops overhead. Best for teams with a data engineer.
- **Stitch (by Talend)** — Lower-cost Singer-based alternative; smaller connector catalog but transparent flat pricing.
- **Matillion** — If your transformation logic is heavy and warehouse-native, Matillion's push-down ELT may fit better than either Fivetran or Hevo.

## FAQ

**Is Hevo Data cheaper than Fivetran?**
At low volumes (under ~10M events/month) Hevo's $299 flat Starter tier is usually cheaper and more predictable. At 100M+ MAR/month, Fivetran's per-MAR model typically wins. Verified April 2026.

**Does Fivetran support real-time sync?**
Not in the sub-minute sense. Fivetran's lowest sync frequency is 1 minute on Enterprise plans; most customers run 15-minute to 24-hour intervals. For true real-time, use a CDC tool like Debezium or Striim.

**Can I use dbt with Hevo Data?**
Yes — Hevo loads raw data to your warehouse, and dbt can run downstream. However, Hevo's own in-UI Python transforms are often used in place of dbt by smaller teams.

**Which has better SAP and Oracle ERP connectors?**
Fivetran, as of April 2026. Fivetran offers native connectors for SAP ECC, SAP HANA, Oracle, and NetSuite. Hevo's ERP coverage is narrower — confirm specific source support with Hevo before purchase.

**How long does migration between them take?**
Typically 1-2 weeks per 10 connectors, assuming both tools support the same sources. Schema normalization differences usually require light adjustments to downstream dbt models.
